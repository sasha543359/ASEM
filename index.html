About us

The IT4BA Incubator was created according to the December 2014 decision of the Senate of the Academy of Economic Studies of Moldova and the July 30, 2015 decision of the Supreme Council for Science and Technological Development of the Academy of Sciences of Moldova.

It is aimed at providing practical assistance and methodical training and upgrading of the intellectual, innovative and educational capacities of incubator and other stakeholders' staff to create innovative start-ups that will contribute to increasing the competitiveness of entrepreneurship in the Republic of Moldova.

Our aims

• In the context of the information society, we are witnessing a dramatic increase in the role of information and communication technologies in the economic development, which proved to be a decisive factor in the development of modern and reliable economic entities.

• Incubator aims to provide practical and methodical assistance for students, postgraduate, doctoral candidates and young professionals in creating innovative start-ups that will help increase the competitiveness of the Republic of Moldova.

• Another important goal - the creation of the educational ecosystem on the basis of the Faculty of Information Technologies and Economic Statistics, Academy of Science of Moldova and the public private partnership with various organizations working in the field of Information Communication Technologies (ICT).

There are established the following objectives:

• Stimulating innovation and technology transfer in order to introduce ICT-based circuit of research results

• Increase the quality and competitiveness of SMEs that rely on ICT

• Supporting ICT strategies in sustainable regional development.

Infrastructure:

  • Existing spaces within ASEM - block B, et. 8, > 1000 m2;
  • 8 residential locations in the field (ICT) formed on the basis of the contest;
  • Three laboratories: MS IT Academy and application software (eg automated bookkeeping), a specialized e-commerce and internet marketing laboratory, a geographic information systems laboratory (GIS);
  • Modern computer room (18) equipped with docking room 2 projectors and transferable screens, audio system, wall board;
  • Team building room (for 25 seats) equipped with projector and furniture board;
  • Conference room 50 seats;
  • Meeting room for 8 people with scanner, color printer, doc camera;
  • Black and white A3 printer;
  • Black and white A4 size printer;

Services:

  • Mentoring, Advice (legal, business, etc.)
  • Special educational short time and long time programs oriented to information society development
  • Office space for Start UP teams, renting fees lower than those of the market
  • Administrative and technical services (telephone, fax, conference rooms, training, secretary services, etc.)
  • Briefings, technology transfer activities, seminars, professional training, etc.

Facilities for residents:

  • Assisting and consulting new entrepreneurs to organize the work and build an innovative business model
  • Providing professional advisers in economic, information technology, management, and legal field
  • Meeting room, StartUpRoom equipped with fax, telephone, and projector
