Our aims

• In the context of the information society, we are witnessing a dramatic increase in the role of information and communication technologies in the economic development, which proved to be a decisive factor in the development of modern and reliable economic entities.

• Incubator aims to provide practical and methodical assistance for students, postgraduate, doctoral candidates and young professionals in creating innovative start-ups that will help increase the competitiveness of the Republic of Moldova.

• Another important goal - the creation of the educational ecosystem on the basis of the Faculty of Information Technologies and Economic Statistics, Academy of Science of Moldova and the public private partnership with various organizations working in the field of Information Communication Technologies (ICT).
